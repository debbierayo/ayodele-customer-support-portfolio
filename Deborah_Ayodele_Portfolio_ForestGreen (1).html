Re: Exchange rate on my last conversion
Currency conversion rate confusion

Hi Tolu, totally fair question, the rate on your receipt and the rate you saw earlier in the app aren't going to match exactly, and I get why that's confusing.

The rate shown in-app is a live estimate that updates constantly. The rate you're actually charged is locked in the moment you confirm, so if a few seconds passed, the market rate could've shifted slightly. Yours moved by about 0.3%, which is normal, not a fee or markup on our end.

If you want, I can walk you through where to see the locked-in rate before you confirm next time. Just say the word.
